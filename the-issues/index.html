Councilman Gary Gardner's Priorities

My number one priority for the next four years is simple: KEEP THE MOMENTUM GOING!

Desert Hot Springs is the fasted-growing city in the Coachella Valley, and we need to keep up with that growth. In the last four years this city has made remarkable strides. For a long time, much of the valley, and sadly a number of our own residents have looked down their noses at us. No longer are they doing that. People are noticing Desert Hot Springs for the RIGHT reasons now:

  • The lowest crime rate in the valley thanks to our outstanding Police Department
  • Nearly 800 new homes built or being built, with the lowest median home price in the desert.
  • A thriving tourism sector centered on our amazing boutique spas and hot mineral water.
  • Major new employers like Amazon, and an expanding cannabis industry

Where people used to say “DHS????”
we’ve turned that around to say “DH YES!!”

We also need to continue to address the other ongoing issues that will make our lives better:

  • More park space, and not just grassy areas in the city. We have the most conservation lands of any city in the desert, and as chair of the Coachella Valley Conservation Commission, I’ll work to redefine what we can do with that open space – how we can responsibly enjoy it and protect our environment.
  • Making sure our Police Department grows with the community.
  • Keeping our spa industry healthy and growing.
  • Keeping our municipal finances on track and continuing to add to our reserves for future years.
  • Making the city more attractive to new retail and restaurants.

Gary Gardner for DHS

Working to keep Desert Hot Springs safe, fiscally responsible, business friendly, and moving forward.
