Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about Gary Gardner, his priorities, and how you can get involved in building a stronger Desert Hot Springs.

What Residents Are Asking

We’ve gathered answers to some of the most common questions about Gary Gardner’s leadership, priorities, and campaign. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to reach out — we’re always happy to connect.

Who is Gary Gardner?

Gary Gardner is a member of the Desert Hot Springs City Council representing District 1. He was first elected in 2018 and re-elected in 2022. He has also served on the Planning Commission and chaired the Human Rights Committee. Learn more about Gary.

What district does Gary Gardner represent?

Gary Gardner represents District 1 in Desert Hot Springs, including the area south of Pierson Boulevard and west of Palm Drive.

What are Gary Gardner’s priorities for Desert Hot Springs?

His priorities include public safety, responsible growth, fiscal responsibility, tourism, parks and conservation, and making Desert Hot Springs more attractive to businesses, retail, and restaurants. Explore the issues and priorities.

What has Gary Gardner helped accomplish?

The campaign highlights lower crime rates, full police staffing, a new fire station, downtown murals and events, Hacienda Avenue improvements, Eagle Sports Park, tourism support, jobs from new employers, and trail improvements. See major accomplishments.

How has Desert Hot Springs improved in recent years?

The site describes Desert Hot Springs as one of the fastest-growing cities in the Coachella Valley, with rising tourism, new housing, more jobs, stronger public safety, and continued investment in infrastructure and community spaces. Read more about recent progress.

What is Gary Gardner’s position on public safety?

Public safety is a core focus. The site emphasizes lower crime rates, full police staffing, improved dispatch services, and new fire station investment to keep residents safe. Read more about public safety priorities.

What is Gary Gardner’s position on growth and development?

Gary Gardner’s message emphasizes balancing responsible growth with prudent fiscal management while supporting tourism, cannabis, housing, and business development. Read more about growth and development.

What is Gary Gardner doing for parks, trails, and open space?

The campaign highlights Eagle Sports Park, trailhead improvements, conservation work, and a focus on using and protecting open space responsibly. See parks and trails accomplishments.

How can I support Gary Gardner’s campaign?

You can help by endorsing the campaign, donating, following and sharing on social media, and encouraging other residents to get involved. Support the campaign or make a donation.

How can I contact Gary Gardner’s campaign?

You can contact the campaign by phone, email, or through the website contact page. Visit the contact page.

Why is Gary Gardner running for re-election?

Gary Gardner’s campaign message is to keep the momentum going and continue building on progress in public safety, infrastructure, economic growth, tourism, and quality of life in Desert Hot Springs.

Working to keep Desert Hot Springs safe, fiscally responsible, business friendly, and moving forward.
