Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Information about how this website collects, uses, and protects personal information.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their personally identifiable information is being used online. Personally identifiable information, as described in United States privacy and information security law, is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate an individual.

What personal information we collect

When using this website, visitors may be asked to provide information such as a name, email address, mailing address, phone number, occupation, donation information, or other details relevant to forms submitted through the site.

When we collect information

We collect information when you fill out a form, submit contact details, make a donation inquiry, endorse the campaign, volunteer, or otherwise enter information on the website.

How we use your information

We may use the information collected from you in the following ways:

  • To respond to messages, questions, endorsements, volunteer requests, and donation inquiries.
  • To send campaign updates, informational emails, or other communications related to the website or campaign.
  • To follow up after correspondence by email, phone, or website form submission.
  • To improve the user experience and maintain the functionality of the website.

How we protect your information

Personal information submitted through this website is maintained using reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. Access to submitted information is limited to authorized persons who need that information for campaign and website administration purposes.

Sensitive payment transactions are not stored or processed directly on this website. When credit card donations are enabled, payments are processed through a third-party provider such as Stripe.

Cookies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, remember preferences, and gather aggregate site traffic information. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.

Third-party disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personally identifiable information to outside parties except as needed to operate the website, process donations through authorized service providers, comply with legal obligations, or protect rights and security.

Third-party links and services

This website may link to third-party websites or use third-party services such as Netlify, Stripe, embedded video platforms, analytics tools, or social media platforms. Those services have their own privacy practices and policies.

Google and analytics

This website may use analytics and related tools to better understand site traffic and visitor interactions. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to provide aggregate information about how visitors use the site.

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

In accordance with CalOPPA, users can visit this site anonymously. A link to this privacy policy is made available on the site, and users will be notified of material policy changes by updates posted on this page.

Do Not Track

This site honors Do Not Track signals where reasonably supported and does not knowingly override browser-based Do Not Track preferences.

Children’s privacy

This website is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly market to or collect personal information from children under 13.

Data breach response

If a data breach affecting personal information is discovered, we will take reasonable steps to investigate, contain, and notify affected users when appropriate and required by law.

CAN-SPAM compliance

When sending email communications, we agree to:

  • Not use false or misleading subjects or email addresses.
  • Identify campaign or promotional messages appropriately.
  • Provide a means to opt out or unsubscribe from future email communications.
  • Honor unsubscribe requests promptly.

Terms of Service

By accessing and using this website, you agree to use it only for lawful purposes and in a way that does not infringe the rights of, restrict, or inhibit the use of this website by any other person.

All website content is provided for general informational and campaign communication purposes. While reasonable efforts are made to keep information current and accurate, no guarantee is made that all content is complete, accurate, or up to date at all times.

We reserve the right to update, modify, suspend, or discontinue any portion of this website at any time without prior notice.

Unauthorized use of this website, including attempts to disrupt site operations, misuse forms, or access restricted systems, may result in legal action.
